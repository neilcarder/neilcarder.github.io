About.

I have been interested in designing spaces since I was in my teens. I began with architecture and am using this website to broaden my knowledge of software development; I think it would be great to one day bring the two skills together as I see the further consolidation of both fields in the future! I'm recording my experiences in this blog.

Formal Education

June-August, 2020 - Online Web Development Course at IBAT College in Dublin


2008-2010 Arts Honors degree in history and Theory of Architecture at Carleton University (Left this degree once accepted into architecture)


2010-2014 Bachelor of Architectural Studies(design) with Distinction. Azrieli School ofArchitecture and Urbanism (Carleton University)


2013 Participated in exchange program with University of Leeds (United Kingdom) January—June, 2013

Informal Education

June, 2020 - I decided to build my own website from scratch in order to learn some basics about software development and help market myself. There have been a number of websites and apps that have helped me as well as the IBAT web developer course that has showed me the basics of web development.


Please take a look at my blog showing my experience of learning the basics of software development.

Work Experience

References Available Upon Request

September 30th, 2019 — June 1, 2020
Architectural Assistant at John Fleming Architects - Dublin, Ireland
Primarily working on planning applications and feasibility studies in relation to Dublin city Council and surrounding areas(Kildare County Council). Schemes include Build to rent, build to sell (SHD Applications), student accommodation and hotel schemes. Housing types vary from terrace houses, duplexes and apartment blocks. Hotel designs are based inside and outside Dublin city centre. Currently using programs such as AutoCAD, Sketchup, Photoshop & InDesign.


November, 2019
Secondary Resource as Copywriter - Remazing - Remote
Briefly offered a chance in performing freelance copy-writing services on Amazon online products in the Canadian market. Used a combination of previous copy and key search words to create product descriptions.


October 15th, 2018 — August 31, 2019
Partner Support Agent at SELLBYTEL Barcelona - Barcelona, Spain
Provided Business to Business support via email for Fortune 500 Companies. Main goal was to onboard companies to resell various software. Applicants are guided through a specific process while completing tasks such as Credit Checks, Due Diligence applications and Contract Signatures. Developed a well rounded understanding of products as many questions were raised by partners during the onboarding process. Gained troubleshooting experience as well as case management skills.

October 26, 2015—August 31, 2018
Draftsperson/designer at ICON Architects - Toronto, Ontario, Canada
ICON is a small firm that allowed me to work in a wide variety of positions. My position ranged from working on schematic design and site plan approvals to submitting building permits and going on monthly site visits (contract admin). Envelope/site design is done on Google Sketchup while the remaining design work is done on AutoCAD. Work also involved coordination with a wide variety of consultants to submit fully developed projects in a timely manner.


June, 2017
Toronto of the Future.
Managed and designed two panel layouts for the Toronto of the Future Exhibit (for ICON Architects).


May, 2013—October 2016
Skilled Labor at Cambro Contracting/ CREW Construction Newmarket, Ontario, Canada
Tasks varied from installing trim to framing/sheeting/shingling a roof. Various tasks were left for me to undertake by myself while other tasks required teamwork.

Programs
Modeling

Rhinocerous

Can create complex shapes and detailed models; can render materials/lighting (using vray); transfer “cuts” of drawing into AutoCAD to create floor plans. Many projects in my bachelors degree was done using Rhinoceros.

Revit

Currently developing my understanding of Revit. Able to model and render in this program. Can add walls, doors, roof, windows, etc. Familiar with create form tool. Able to create section views and plan views.

AutoCAD

Three and half years working experience at ICON and JFA. Used to create schematic designs, permit drawings and presentation panels.

Google Sketchup

Used in both ICON and JFA Office. Used for making AutoCAD designs into a 3D form. Designs are used primarily for presentation purposes (to clients or for submission to city).

Graphics

Adobe Photoshop

Very familiar with this program. All of my renderings are taken into Photoshop. I also use Photoshop for photography; I use things like cropping, colour, contrast , photo merging, etc.

Adobe Illustrator

I use Illustrator for any graphic design/panel layout. Sections and plans are imported into Illustrator in order to create diagrams. Created icons and graphics using illustrator

Adobe InDesign

Recently gained experience in this program at John Fleming Architects for creating Architectural design statements.

Adobe XD

Recently used Adobe XD to develop a base design for this website. Used artboards to develop the layout and design. Then used a variety of other programs to build the site for web.
